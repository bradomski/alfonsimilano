Our Story

Alfonsi Milano is a luxury handmade footwear company based in Napoli, Italy, offering premium 'affordable luxury' menswear since 2019.

We partner with B2B retail stores, online and offline, to elevate offerings with exquisite handcrafted Italian footwear.

Market Opportunity

Our target customers are aged 25 to 55, with high disposable income, seeking the perfect blend of comfort and elegance.

Product Overview

We offer a diverse range, from classic to forward styles, crafted from the finest materials to ensure satisfaction and repeat business.

Vision & Mission

We aim to be the go-to brand for retail stores seeking high-quality handcrafted footwear. Our mission is to empower retailers with an exclusive collection of comfortable, handmade, high-end men's shoes, blending tradition and modernity.
